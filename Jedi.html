Yoda first appears in the 1980 film The Empire Strikes Back. He's known for his legendary wisdom, mastery of the Force and skills in lightsaber combat. Grand Master Yoda spent eight centuries training Jedi. He served as a member of the Jedi Council in the last centuries of the Galactic Republic and oversaw the Jedi Order as Grand Master, during and after the devastating Clone Wars.
